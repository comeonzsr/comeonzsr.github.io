The Geographic Information and Analysis Laboratory (GIAL)

The GIAL is a multipurpose facility shared by the Department of Geography and the National Center for Geographic Information and Analysis (NCGIA). The laboratory supports the teaching and research needs of Geography faculty and staff, graduate, and undergraduate studies.
